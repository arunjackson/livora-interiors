The process

How We Work

A transparent design journey from consultation to handover.

01

Free Site Visit

We visit your property to understand your space, take measurements, and assess the potential.

02

Free Consultation

We discuss your lifestyle, ideas, inspirations, and what you envision for your dream space.

03

Understanding Your Requirements

We dive deep into your needs, preferences, and functional requirements to ensure perfect alignment.

04

Budget Planning

We help you plan your budget strategically, ensuring maximum value for your investment.

05

Commitment Towards Project

We charge 2% of budget value as commitment towards project which shall be adjusted in the project cost.

06

Preparing 2D Layout

We create detailed 2D floor plans that optimize space utilization and flow.

07

3D Visualisation

We bring your space to life with realistic 3D renderings showing every detail before execution.

08

Preparing Quotation

We provide a clear BOQ with transparent pricing and detailed material specifications.

09

Approval & Advance Payment

After approval, we collect 50% advance to commence work and secure materials.

10

Completion of Structure

We complete all structural work including false ceiling, electrical, plumbing, and carpentry.

11

Interim Payment

40% payment at the time of structure completion to continue with finishing work.

12

Final Handover

Remaining 10% payment and completed home handover, ready to be lived in.
